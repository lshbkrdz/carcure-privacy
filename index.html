CarCure Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 2025

CarCure is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information in the CarCure mobile app.

Information We Collect

  • Camera access: Used only to take photos of vehicle damage for analysis.
  • Storage access: Allows saving and uploading photos you choose to analyze.
  • Location access: Helps suggest nearby repair shops and provide regional price estimates.

How We Use Your Information

All data collected is used solely for app functionality and improving the accuracy of repair cost estimations. We do not sell, rent, or share personal information with third parties.

Data Security

We take appropriate measures to secure your data against unauthorized access or disclosure.

Data Retention

Photos and location data are not stored permanently and are deleted after analysis unless you choose to save them.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns, contact us at support@carcure.com.

By using CarCure, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

Account Deletion Request

If you wish to delete your CarCure account and all associated data, please contact us at support@carcure.com. Your request will be processed within 7 business days. All photos, account information, and location data will be permanently deleted from our servers.
